Chicken Curry

Picture of Chicken Curry

A chicken curry is a popular dish among south asian communities.

List of Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cooking Oil
  • Curry Powder
  • Bay Leaves
  • Red Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Ginger-garlic Sauce
  • Tomatoes

Procedure:

  1. In a thick bottom skillet pour oil and let it simmer
  2. Cook diced red onions until light brown
  3. Turn the heat down to medium and pour in ginger-garlic sauce
  4. Cook squared chicken breast pieces in medium heat
  5. Add spices: curry powder, salt, pepper, chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala
  6. Cover the skillet for 5-6 minutes
  7. Add sliced tomatoes
  8. Cover the skillet for 5-6 minutes
  9. Pour in water for gravy
  10. Cook for 35-40 minutes in medium heat
